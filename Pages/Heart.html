Of One Heart

This dance piece explores the enduring connection between two souls—whether siblings or strangers—bound by an invisible thread that neither time nor distance can sever. Performed on a stage hoop, the dancers are connected by a cord symbolizing the umbilical connection. As one tries to escape, the other pulls closer, embodying the ebb and flow of relationships. The cord becomes a lifeline, a reminder that no matter how far they run or change, the connection remains—sometimes strained, sometimes distant, but never broken.


Karim Ennoury

Karim Ennoury is a UAE-based dancer, choreographer, actor, and singer. He has a degree from the High Institute of Dramatic Arts in Morocco and Sharjah Performing Arts Academy where he also teaches the Young Performers Programme. To name a few, Karim has worked on projects and events such as Dibba Festival, Sharjah Theatre Festival, Tunes DXB and Expo.

Ksenia Klubova

Ksenia Klubova is a UAE-based dancer, choreographer and aerialist. Together with Karim, she is one of the pioneering graduates from Sharjah Performing Arts Academy. Ksenia has been a part of projects like Al Basheer Show, Lebanon Pole Championship and the Short and Sweet Festival, among others. She is a renowned dance and aerial teacher in Dubai, and leads workshops globally.
